Jesper teaches at the intersection of statistics, business analytics, and artificial intelligence. He is programme coordinator for the MSc in Business Intelligence at Aarhus University and teaches graduate courses in generative AI, deep learning, machine learning, and Bayesian data analysis. His teaching has been recognized with the Aarhus BSS Lecturer of the Year award and consistently high student evaluations.

- **AI for Business Intelligence** — MSc in Business Intelligence & MSc in Data Science

## Outreach

Jesper produces teaching materials that bring statistical thinking to a broader audience. His video series on **k-means clustering for high-school students** introduces segmentation through worked examples (in Danish).
